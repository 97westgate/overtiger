Privacy Policy for Fruit Lines

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Introduction

Fruit Lines ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use Fruit Lines, we automatically collect certain information through third-party services:

Google Analytics for Firebase

  • Game events (scores, combos, gameplay actions)
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Google AdMob

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • Device characteristics

Information You Provide

Fruit Lines does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Photos or contacts

How We Use Information

We use collected information for:

  1. Analytics: Understanding how players interact with the game to improve gameplay experience
  2. Advertising: Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  3. Performance: Monitoring app stability and fixing bugs
  4. App Functionality: Ensuring the game runs smoothly on your device

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Analytics for Firebase

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Purpose: Analytics and crash reporting
  • Data collected: Usage data, device information

Google AdMob

  • AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Purpose: Serving advertisements
  • Data collected: Advertising ID, device information, ad interactions

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Google (Firebase & AdMob): For analytics and advertising services
  • Apple: As required for App Store functionality

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information transmitted through our third-party services. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Fruit Lines does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is designed to be family-friendly and does not require personal information from any users.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to your device Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking (iOS)
  • Reset Advertising ID: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Reset Advertising Identifier
  • Delete game data: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

Data Retention

  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 2-14 months)
  • Local game data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app
  • Advertising data: Managed by Google AdMob according to their policies

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

Fruit Lines is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.
