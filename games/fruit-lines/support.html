üéÆ How to Play

Basic Rules

  1. Move Fruit: Tap a fruit, then tap an empty cell to move it
  2. Create Lines: Match 5 or more fruit of the same type in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
  3. Score Points: Each fruit cleared gives you 2 points
  4. Plan Ahead: New fruit appear after each move, so think strategically!

Juice Meter & Power-Ups

The unique Juice Meter fills as you clear lines. When it reaches 100%, you earn a power-up!

  • üßÉ Juice Bomb: Clear ALL fruit of one color from the board
  • You can store up to 2 power-ups at once
  • Tap a power-up, then tap any fruit to use it

Tips for High Scores

  • üéØ Create longer lines (6+ fruit) for more points
  • üß† Plan moves to set up multiple lines at once

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I save my progress?
A: Your high score is automatically saved on your device. No account needed!
Q: How do I get power-ups?
A: Fill the juice meter by clearing lines! Each 100% fills gives you one power-up.
Q: Can I remove ads?
A: Currently, Fruit Lines is ad-supported to keep it free for everyone. We may add an ad-free option in the future!
Q: What happens when the board fills up?
A: Game Over! But don't worry - you can start a new game and try to beat your high score.
Q: How do I turn off sound?
A: Tap the speaker icon in the top-right corner to toggle sound on/off.
Q: Does Fruit Lines have leaderboards?
A: Not yet, but we're considering adding Game Center leaderboards in a future update!
Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?
A: Email us at support@overtiger.com with details about the issue. Screenshots help!

üîß Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. If problem persists, contact us at support@overtiger.com

Ads Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Sometimes ads aren't available - this is normal
  • The game will still work perfectly without ads

Game Won't Start

  • Delete and reinstall the app (your high score is stored locally and will be lost)
  • Make sure you have enough storage space
  • Check for iOS updates
